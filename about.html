About Us

iCREAM is Devoted to deliver the exceptionally best-frozen yogurt and sorbet, iCREAM opened in August 1950 in Old Dehli. Quite a while later, we moved our retail to a bigger space at the Connaught Place (corner of East Houston St) offering a far-reaching window into an open kitchen, where our solidified ice-creams are handmade in little clumps, guaranteeing the most astounding quality. We source the world over for kinds of immaculateness and perfection when conceivable sourcing locally and naturally.
